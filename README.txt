Data collected from a small business owner to analyse trends and gain insights.
Analyzing 3 years of order details.
Data cleaning and data manipulation performed using MS Excel.
Imported this data to R to analyze how each category of products performed over months.
Identified top performing category in individual years by extracting profit analysis.
Created plots to observe trends over months.
Exported the cleaned R dataframe to CSV file.
Imported this on SSMS to query and calculate total revenue, cost and profit details.
Queried to identify top loyal and high value customers.

The data has been collected and published with consent of the data owner. However the business name has been changed for anonimity purposes.
